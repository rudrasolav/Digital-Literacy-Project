# Social Media Do's and Don'ts Checklist

## Do's
 Be respectful and polite in your posts and comments.
 Protect your privacy by adjusting account settings.
 Share useful and meaningful content.
 Verify information before sharing it.
 Maintain a professional online presence.

##  Don'ts
 Do not share personal or sensitive information publicly.
 Avoid posting offensive or inappropriate content.
 Do not spread fake news or misinformation.
 Avoid cyberbullying or negative comments.
 Do not overshare unnecessary details about your life.

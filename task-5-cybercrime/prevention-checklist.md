# Stay Safe Online – Prevention Checklist

## General Safety
- Use strong, unique passwords for each account.
- Turn on two-factor authentication (2FA) for email, social media, and banking apps.
- Do not click on unknown links in SMS, email, WhatsApp, or Instagram messages.
- Check the sender carefully before sharing any personal or account information.
- Keep your phone, apps, and browser updated regularly.
- Avoid logging into important accounts on public Wi-Fi.
- Log out of accounts on shared devices after use.
- Be careful while downloading apps; use only official app stores and trusted sources.

## UPI / Financial Safety
- Never share your UPI PIN, OTP, CVV, or bank password with anyone.
- Remember: to receive money, you do not need to enter your UPI PIN or scan a QR code.
- Do not scan QR codes sent by unknown people or sellers.
- Always check UPI payment requests twice before approving them.
- Use only official payment apps such as Google Pay, PhonePe, Paytm, or your bank’s app.

## Reporting
- If you suspect cybercrime or online fraud, report it at **cybercrime.gov.in**.
- For urgent financial fraud, call **1930** immediately.
